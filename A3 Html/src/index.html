PARASITE

Overview

"Parasite" is a South Korean film that into themes of social inequality, class struggle, and the human condition. The story follows the Kim family, who live in a cramped, semi basement apartment and struggle to make ends meet. Each family member father, mother, son, and daughter is unemployed and facing a bleak future. Their fortunes seem to change when son's friend offers him a lucrative tutoring job for the wealthy Park family. With forged documents and a clever scheme, son's secures the job and soon engineers a plan to employ his entire family within the Park household, under false pretenses. daughter becomes an art therapist for the Parks' son, father becomes the family driver, and momther becomes the housekeeper, all while hiding their true relationships from the Parks. As the Kim family embeds itself into the Parks' luxurious life, they encounter unexpected complications. The former housekeeper returns, revealing a hidden secret in the basement that threatens to unravel their elaborate deception. The story escalates into a tense and unpredictable confrontation, exposing the stark differences between the two families and leading to a violent and tragic conclusion.

Important scene

The underground unit is pivotal in "Parasite," serving as a metaphor for the hidden struggles of the lower class. It dramatically shifts the narrative, leading to the film's climactic and tragic events. This revelation forces the audience to confront the uncomfortable realities of social inequality and the often unseen lives of the impoverished.

Plot Summary

The Kim family, living in a cramped basement apartment, struggle to make ends meet. When the son, Ki-woo, gets a job as an English tutor for the wealthy Park family's daughter, the Kims see an opportunity to improve their situation. They infiltrate the Park household by getting the existing staff fired and replacing them with their own family members. However, their plan leads to unexpected and tragic consequences.

Characters

  • Ki-taek (Song Kang ho) - The father of the Kim family.
  • Chung-sook (Jang Hye jin) - The mother of the Kim family.
  • Ki-woo (Choi Woo shik) - The son who becomes an English tutor.
  • Ki-jung (Park So dam) - The daughter who becomes an art therapist.
  • Mr. Park (Lee Sun kyun) - The wealthy patriarch of the Park family.
  • Mrs. Park (Cho Yeo jeong) - The naive and kind-hearted wife of Mr. Park.
